Crystal Collector Game

How to Play
You will be given a "target score" at the start of the game. There are four crystals below, each with its own numerical value which is discoverable upon click. Each time you click a crystal its value will be added to your total score. Your goal is to determine enough crystal values so you can match your total score to the "target score".
